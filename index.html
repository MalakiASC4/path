WHAT IS "THE PATH"?

To understand what "The Path" is, you must first understand the problem we wish to fix.

The Problem

The problem we're currently facing is that we have alot of uninformed high school students who end up falling short of going to college. They often dropout or end up going to vocational school. Out of this fact, the majority of those dropping out are in the minority (African Americans, Hispanics, etc.)

Now that we understand the problem, let us inform you on the solution.

The Path

The Path is a program that provides students with personalized real time data so they can make informed decisions to reach their academic goals. It relies on an AI guidance counselor to monitor the student's grades and provide feedback for improvement.
